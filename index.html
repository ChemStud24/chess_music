Music for Your Chess Games!

Fetch games from any Chess.com user's archive and review them with a musical narration that adjusts to the current context of the game.

This app was built with node.js, jQuery, chess.js, chessboard.js, and stockfish.js (powered by the open-source chess engine Stockfish) and uses the Chess.com Public API. The music was obtained royalty-free through Dripchord and was composed by the user SKI.
